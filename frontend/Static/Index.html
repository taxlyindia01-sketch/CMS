Core Features

Everything your CA firm needs

A complete lifecycle platform — from first enquiry through incorporation, governance, compliance and statutory registers.

Lead & Client Management
Capture enquiries, auto-generate AI welcome docs, convert leads to clients with unique IDs and automatic staff assignment by workload.
Company Master Records
Post-incorporation database — CIN, directors with DIN, shareholders with folio numbers, register of charges and share transfer history.
Meeting Management
Board Meetings, AGM, EGM — AI generates compliant notices and minutes per Secretarial Standards SS-1/SS-2.
Post-Inc Compliance
Auto-seeded alerts for INC-20A, ADT-1, first board meeting with statutory deadlines, penalty info and AI drafts.
Compliance Reminders
Annual calendar — MGT-7, AOC-4, DIR-3 KYC, DPT-3, MSME-1. Auto due/overdue updates and AI reminder emails.
Statutory Registers
Auto-populated MGT-1, Sec 170, CHG-7 and SH-6 registers. Export as branded PDF or Excel with cover sheet.
